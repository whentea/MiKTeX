### Sections, Subsections, Paragraphs, Subparagraphs

```

\title{Adding sections, subsections, paragraphs, subparagraphs}
\date{May 6, 2019}
\author{whentea}

\begin{document}
\pagenumbering{gobble}
\maketitle

\pagenumbering{arabic}

 Cin is ...

\end{document}
```
